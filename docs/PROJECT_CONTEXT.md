# Project Context

## What is Proovd?

Proovd is an affiliate-powered startup validation platform. It connects early-stage founders who need real market signal with affiliate content creators who promote ideas to their audiences for commission. Money moves only when an audience member pledges — making it one of the cheapest, fastest ways to validate whether a startup idea has genuine demand before building anything.

## Two user types

**Founders** have an idea but no proof anyone will pay. They face the classic validation trap: people say "I'd buy that" but vanish when checkout appears. Proovd gives them a structured way to test demand in three weeks or less, for as little as $55, without building a product first.

**Affiliates** are content creators with genuine, conversion-proven audiences (40K–250K followers). They want to earn by promoting products that resonate with their community. Proovd gives them pre-vetted early-stage ideas to promote, tiered commissions (20% base, up to 30% if they hit milestones), and a plug-and-play onboarding kit so they can go live in minutes.

## How it works (plain terms)

1. **Founder submits an idea.** They fill a lightweight vetting form (problem, solution, competition, optional visuals, branding, interview, story, socials) and pay a $55–75 listing fee.
2. **Algorithm matches affiliates.** The platform finds affiliates whose audiences fit the idea's niche. If no match is found within 48–72 hours, the founder gets a near-full refund.
3. **Affiliate accepts, campaign starts.** Once an affiliate commits, they get a unique affiliate link plus a full promotional kit (pre-written posts, video scripts, brand guide). The campaign runs up to three weeks.
4. **Backers pledge.** Audience members who click through pledge money. They answer a short survey so the founder gets real qualitative feedback, not just numbers.
5. **Affiliate gets paid immediately.** Commission is credited per pledge in real time — no waiting on founder milestones.
6. **Founder gets funded conditionally.** If the campaign hits its goal, the founder receives an initial 20% disbursement to start building an MVP, then the remainder after showing progress within one month.

## Current project scope

This repo is **the marketing landing page only** at `proovd.io`. It is a pre-launch page whose single job is to capture interest from both founders and affiliates and drive sign-ups for early access.

The product MVP (app.proovd.io) is a separate repo and out of scope here.

## Launch niche

AI startups and AI content creators. US market, California-first for regulatory simplicity. Expansion to fashion, fitness, and consumer products follows as success stories accumulate.
